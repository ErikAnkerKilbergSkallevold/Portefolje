The all-light shoe lets you show of all your colours. Experience the pinnacle of LED technology with light-up soles, light-up fabric and light-up shoe laces. Our “light-up-sport-condenser” fabric is first in class and trendsetter for all light-up shoes to come. We use only the highest quality double backed, hard-through LEDs from Germany. Experience over 10000 LEDs, more than 4k resolution on your shoes. Use the mobile app to control your LEDs and to use the pedometer. The all-light shoes still retain the durability of our other shoes, whilst delivering even better sweat transportation and breathability for your shoe. Truly light up your world!
